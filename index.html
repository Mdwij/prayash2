About Prayash

Prayash heavy engineering, established in May 2022, has swiftly gained customer trust with its focus on delivering top-quality work and timely services, fostering enduring business relationships with stakeholders. Since the inception, we are committed to manufacturing products that meet international standards. We provide a comprehensive range of fabricated and machined products like Pressure vessel, reactors, Tanks & Silos, Skid packages, Piping and Other Structural Steel fabrication.
We've attentively built every aspect of production to ensure zero compromise quality. Our infrastructure has the latest manufacturing technology and machines. The highly skilled team is capable of producing in huge quantities at a fast production rate.
